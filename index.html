Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our products, process, and how we work.

How does HistOracle make AI conversations historically accurate?

HistOracle uses advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines built on curated historical document corpora. Every response is grounded in verified sources, so conversations with historical figures stay factually rooted rather than relying on generic AI hallucinations.

What problem does API2Gov solve for businesses?

Navigating US business licenses and permits is fragmented across thousands of jurisdictions. API2Gov consolidates this into a single API — giving compliance teams, legal platforms, and business formation services instant access to licensing requirements nationwide.

Do you take on custom projects or only build your own products?

Selectively, yes. We partner on projects that align with our core expertise in AI, education, and civic tech. If the problem is meaningful and the technical challenge is interesting, we're open to collaborating. Reach out through the contact form to start a conversation.

What kind of support do you provide after launch?

We don't build and walk away. Every product gets ongoing monitoring, performance optimization, and iterative feature development based on real user feedback. We treat launch as the starting line, not the finish.

Where is EditLingo based and how big is the team?

We're based in New Jersey, USA. We're a small, lean team by design — every engineer ships to production. This means less overhead, faster decisions, and direct accountability on every product we build.

How can I explore a partnership or collaboration?

We're actively open to partnerships in education, government, and AI infrastructure. Whether it's a joint venture, an integration, or a strategic collaboration — use the contact form below or email us at support@editlingo.com and we'll set up a call.
